Our Projects

We are constantly working on new projects to improve and expand our Daily Expense Management system. Here are some of the key projects we are currently working on:

  • Mobile Application Development

    We are developing a mobile application to allow users to manage their expenses on the go. The app will feature real-time expense tracking, budget management, and financial reporting, all accessible from your smartphone.

  • AI-Powered Expense Categorization

    Our team is working on integrating artificial intelligence to automatically categorize your expenses. This will save you time and improve the accuracy of your expense tracking.

  • Cloud-Based Data Backup

    We are implementing a cloud-based data backup solution to ensure that your financial data is always safe and accessible. This feature will provide you with peace of mind, knowing that your data is securely stored and can be retrieved at any time.

  • Multi-Currency Support

    Our system is being updated to support multiple currencies, making it easier for users who travel frequently or have expenses in different currencies to manage their finances efficiently.
